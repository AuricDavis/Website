Skills for IT

In IT, there are many skills that you will need. Some of them are specific to IT, some of then general skills. Here we will break them down.

    General Skills

  • Communication

    • In IT, you are going to be working with people who know nothing about technology. It is SO important to communicate to them, in a way they understand, what the issue is.

  • Trustworthy

    • In IT, you're probably going to have login information and other things for your clients. If you are not trustworthy, then they are not going to give you what you need to help them.

  • Problem Solving

    • In IT, you are going to be faced with issues that you may not have seen before. You should be able to find ways to solve those problems

  • Adaptability

    • In IT, things are going to change. It's the nature of technology. Things evolve. Because of that, you need to be able to adapt to the new situations.

  • Positive Attitude

    • In IT, you're going to pull you hair. It just comes with the job. If you have a positive attitude, you will not go bald quite as fast as if you didn't. You'll still go bald, though.

    IT Specific Skills

  • Programming

    • Programming is a very good skill to have in any computer field. In IT, it can help you get a job because it will look really good on a resume, and it will help you solve many problems.

  • Powershell

    • Powershell is the life blood of any IT guy/gal. I can't stress this enough. Learn Powershell. It will help you so much in your career. It will save that fleshy part you sit on so many times.

  • Hardware Knowledge

    • How can you be in IT without knowing about hardware? You will literally be fixing it for your job. You need to have the hardware knowledge so you can actually know what you're talking about.

  • Virtual Machines

    • Virtual Machines are good. I don't know exactly why at this moment, but we have a wonderful teacher who can explain it to us and we can start a good discussion :).

  • Eyeballs

    • I don't believe that you need eyeballs to do IT. You can do it by sound and feel. So I don't know why I put this except for the fact my group wanted me to add it while I was gone. Thanks guys, you Bakas
